Welcome to Boxing Dice!

The user and computer each roll a pair of dice 3 times.

After the third roll of the dice, the player with the highest score wins!

Rules

SCORE = 0
If any of the players two dice comes up as a 1 then the score for that round for the player is 0.

(If the player rolls a 6 and 1, they get a score of 0)
